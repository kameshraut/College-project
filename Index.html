About Us

AITRC Chhindwara

AITRC is One of the INDIA's emerging technological institutions, AITRC is a vibrant, connected community that is home to diverse, ambitious, and creative students. It offers an incredible array of academic programs; a diverse, committed, and accessible faculty; sophisticated facilities; and an unusual emphasis in experiential learning.

Welcome to Annie Institute Of Technology & Research Centre - [AITRC], Chhindwara We are dedicated to fostering a better educational experience for students, institutions, and educators alike. Our mission is to bridge the gap between colleges and their students, providing a platform for feedback and insights that can drive positive change within educational institutions.

AITRC is in collaboration with Google to provide Online classes to AITRC students. With Google classroom, faculty can create online classes, distribute assignments, grade and send feedbak, and see everything in one place.
